All of you

I just don't hate that
there's no future of us
I hate that I won't be
The one holding your hands

I hate that I wouldn't be
The one waking you up
I hate that I won't be
The one making tea
Even on summer afternoon

I hate that I cannot be
The one watching you
Doing your workouts
I hate that I couldn't be
The one claiming all your abs

I hate that I won't be
The first to see your art
I hate that I wouldn't be
The one knowing all of you

I hate that I won't be
The one next to you
I hate that you are not
The one I belong to

I hate that now I'm
Here without you
