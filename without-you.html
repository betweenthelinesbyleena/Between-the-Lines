Without You

Once, I needed no one to put myself
Together amidst all the chaos.
I saw myself pushing harder,
Praying and wishing for the best.

But now, I feel different.
I can't move any longer,
Stuck in the corner, hugging
My knees, struggling to breathe.

But one thing that holds me together
Is the guilt, the blame, and the void.
With these, I can't think—but I live.
Since I lost you, I lost me; now,
I can't recognize myself.
