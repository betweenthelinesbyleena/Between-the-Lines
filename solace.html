Solace

One day I sat there, dripping wet,
Hoping my heaviness would eventually
blend with water and find a place
in ocean, submerging the burden.

Other day I sat with the trees,
Wishing my happiness will spread
through the air, bringing smile and
cheers, as the birds softly sing.

And I saw myself sitting under sun,
Unfortunately, to heat up even more.
Then I could say everything from heart
and blame my anger for their tears.

One time I find myself waiting
for the sun to shine, believing
my desperation will fade along
with the moon soothing my soul.
