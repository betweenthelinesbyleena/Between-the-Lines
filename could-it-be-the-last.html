Could It Be the Last

When smile blooms into laughter
When quiet worries spill as tears
As all the miles disappear
Now you get closer and closer

You stop hiding what you feel
Fights become regular
Soothing becomes routine

All this walking toward
that known ending
Yet neither of you can let go

Even though the same question lingers:

Could it be the last.
